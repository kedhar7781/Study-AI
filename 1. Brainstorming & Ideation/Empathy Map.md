# Phase 1: Empathy Map

This empathy map captures the target learner persona's experience when preparing for exams, highlighting how **StudyAI** matches their psychological needs.

---

## 1. User Persona: Busy University Student

### Say
- "I have too much to read before Monday's midterm."
- "I highlighted the whole chapter but I still don't remember the key concepts."
- "I wish I had a quick practice test to check if I am ready."

### Think
- *I am falling behind on my study schedule.*
- *Is this detail going to be on the exam?*
- *I need a better way to test myself rather than just re-reading.*

### Do
- Re-reads pages multiple times passively.
- Searches online for random mock test questions.
- Postpones planning until the day before the test.

### Feel
- Stressed and overwhelmed by content volume.
- Anxious about testing performance.
- Frustrated by disorganized notes.

---

## 2. Pains & Gains Map

### Pains (To minimize)
- Wasting hours compiling manual summaries.
- Getting stuck on complex definitions.
- Feeling lost on where to start studying.

### Gains (To maximize)
- Instantly extracting terms and memory tricks from any PDF.
- Simulating real quiz conditions with immediate grading reports.
- Having a personalized calendar plan indicating study focus.
